Created with Aili Harmon, Caroline Dunn, and Gwen Lynch for CS 4278 at Vanderbilt University in Spring 2023.

For our semester-long Software Engineering Project, we created an event management mobile application for the on-campus organization Studio V. Studio V is a registered student-run club at Vanderbilt University, and their main goal is to create and maintain a women’s health and wellness community within the student population. Their vision to foster a strong fitness community is mainly achieved through their signature off-campus events, where students can explore various workout styles at local studios in Nashville completely free of charge. They also complete a wide variety of on-campus events – including wellness sessions involving yoga, meditation, crafts, or healthy eating.

More details about our project are available on the App Store! Feedback is appreciated – we're really happy to see the project in this state and cannot wait for StudioV to use it moving forward.
